Station D: Platform Security

Women Platform Safety Core

Dedicated neural networks designed to protect female commuters on isolated stations through gesture recognition and thermal stress verification.

Module 01

Handwave Visual SOS Detection

Traditional panic buttons are hard to reach in emergency situations. Our platform-mounted cameras recognize **overhead handwave gestures** from a passenger facing the lens.

  • **Instant SOS Activation:** Eliminates the need to find call boxes or access smartphones.
  • **Visual Handwave AI:** Tracks skeleton vectors and alerts dispatch within 1.5 seconds.
  • **Local Sirens & Announcer:** Instantly triggers platform warning sirens and lights.

Module 02

Thermal Heat & CCTV Sensor Fusion

CCTV systems can fail to detect stress in blind spots or bad lighting. We fuse **Thermal FLIR cameras** with standard optical cameras to monitor platform corners.

  • **Heat Signature Tracking:** Detects abnormal body temperature spikes (stress v. environment signature).
  • **CCTV Auto-Pivot:** Nearest optical camera zooms and pivots automatically onto the thermal anomaly location in seconds.
  • **Station Master Notification:** Transmits optical live pictures to the Station Master desk.
